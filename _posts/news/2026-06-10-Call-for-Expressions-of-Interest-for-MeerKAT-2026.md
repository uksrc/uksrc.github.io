The [South African Radio Astronomy Observatory (SARAO)](https://www.sarao.ac.za/) has announced a new call for observing proposals using the MeerKAT radio telescope.

A minimum of 2,000 hours of observing time will be awarded through this opportunity. MeerKAT comprises 64 dishes with exceptional sensitivity and baselines of up to 8 km, supporting continuum, spectral line, and pulsar/fast transient observations across the UHF, L-band, and S-band receivers.

The deadline for proposal submission is **12:00pm** UTC on **Wednesday 15 July 2026**.

Prospective users are strongly encouraged to review the call documentation and proposal preparation guidance before submitting an application.

## Information Sessions: ##

To support proposal preparation, SARAO will host two online information sessions on 29 June 2026. These sessions will provide an overview of the proposal process, available resources, and an opportunity to ask questions.

## How to Apply:

Researchers interested in applying for observing time are encouraged to attend either session and review the supporting documentation available through the MeerKAT Knowledge Base.
For full details, proposal documentation, and registration information, please refer to the [official SARAO call documentation](https://skaafrica.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/ESDKB/pages/3080126470/2026+Call+for+Proposals).
